St Ives
Community Choir

A fun and relaxed choir based in St. Ives (Cambridgeshire)
welcoming singers of all ages and abilities.

WHAT TYPE OF MUSIC DO YOU PERFORM?

We perform a wide variety of music including Pop, Folk, Rock, Musical Theatre, and occasionally Classical and Gospel. Our MD is always open to song suggestions and concert ideas.

CAN I JOIN?

YES!
We welcome people of all ages and abilities. You don't have to read music and you definitely don't have to audition. Just turn up one week or contact us if you have any questions.

WHEN DO YOU REHEARSE?

Every Monday evening 19:30-21:00 (including school holidays and bank holidays) at the Methodist Church, 4 The Waits, Saint Ives PE27 5BY

HOW MUCH DOES IT COST?

Your first visit is complimentary. Should you choose to join us, there is a fee of £18 per month, paid by Standing Order or cheque if you prefer.
